Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Budget Tracker for Trello ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Trello Power-Up.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and store the following information:

  • Budget Data: Allocated amounts, spent amounts, notes, and timestamps you enter for your Trello cards
  • Trello Board Data: Card names, list names, and board structure to display budget summaries
  • Settings: Your currency preference and notification settings

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Display budget information on your Trello cards, lists, and boards
  • Calculate budget totals and percentages
  • Generate budget reports and CSV exports
  • Save your user preferences and settings

4. Data Storage

All budget data is stored using Trello's built-in data storage system. We do not maintain separate databases or servers that store your personal information. Your data remains within Trello's infrastructure and is subject to Trello's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your budget information with anyone outside your Trello workspace
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Access your data for any purpose other than providing the Power-Up functionality

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Data is stored securely within Trello's infrastructure
  • We follow Trello's security best practices for Power-Up development

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your budget data at any time through the Power-Up interface
  • Delete your budget data by removing it from individual cards or disabling the Power-Up
  • Export your budget data using the CSV export feature
  • Disable the Power-Up at any time from your Trello board settings

8. Data Retention

Budget data is retained as long as you have the Power-Up enabled on your Trello board. When you disable or remove the Power-Up, your budget data remains in Trello's storage but is no longer accessible through our interface.

9. Third-Party Services

This Power-Up integrates with:

  • Trello: Subject to Atlassian's Privacy Policy
  • Trello Power-Up API: For accessing card and board data

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@feedblox.app

13. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, we comply with GDPR requirements:

  • We process data based on your consent when you use the Power-Up
  • You can withdraw consent by disabling the Power-Up
  • You have the right to data portability (CSV export)
  • You can request data deletion by removing budget data from your cards
